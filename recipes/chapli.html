Chapali Kabab

Description

Chapli kebab is the king of all kababs. It has a crunchy texture and tangy and nutty taste. It is full of aromas and bursting with flavors

Ingredients

  • Ground Chicken
  • Ginger and Gatlic Paste
  • Yellow Onion
  • Pomegranate Seeds
  • Jalapeno Peppers
  • Salt
  • Red Pepper
  • Coriander Seeds

Steps

  1. In amedium bowl combine all ingredients except cooking oil spray, and knead well. Set aside for 10 minutes.

  2. Divide mixture into8 partsto make round, flat patties, 2 inches in diameter. Set aside for 5 minutes.

  3. In a medium skilletover medium-low heat, evenly spray Pan-fry four patties for 3 to 5 minutes. Spray cooking oil for an additional 4 seconds on the top of patties and flip to cook for another 3 to 5 minutes.
  4. Repeatthe above steps with remaining four patties
